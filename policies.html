Financing

Even though we are a non-profit, partial payment may be necessary if donations are insufficient. Contact one of our representatives to create an individualized financing plan

Delivery

Delivery will be determined on a case-by-case basis. If you are in the United States or Europe, shipping of textbooks and supplies will be in 3-5 business days. If you are in another country, shipping may take longer.

Return Policy

If any of your products have been compromised, free returns and refunds are offered if the funds allow. While we would like to offer free refunds to everybody, our operation is dependent on donations.
